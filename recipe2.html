Recipe 2: Chicken Tacos!

I choose to make chicken tacos as another recipe because it is another simple recipe that I enjoy.

My Rating of this Recipe: 7/10

I think this was good practice cooking ground chicken and think with I cooked vegetables in a pan before this was good pratice for me of getting better at these simple cooking skills.

Listed below are the ingredients and instructions I followed to make this recipe at Once Upon a Chef.

Ingredients Necessary:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 medium yellow onions
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 4 cloves of garlic (or use minced garlic)
  • 2 pounds of ground chicken
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano/li>
  • 1 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (8oz) can tomato sauce
  • hard or soft taco shells
  • Optional Toppings: shredded Mexican cheese

Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 325Â°F.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add in onions, peppers, and garlic and cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally until soft.
  3. Add chicken and all spices to the saucepan and increase the heat from medium to high. Break the chicken up into smaller pieces ad cook unitl the chicken is partically done. Add in tomato sauce and reduce the heat to low. Occasionally stir until the chicken is fully cooked (may take 12-15 minutes)
  4. Remove taco shells from the packing and lay on a cookie sheet in the oven and bake for 6-7 minutes or until crisp.
  5. Add in cilantro to the pan, and spoon mixture into the taco shells. Add additional topping if you want.
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
